## Before You Start / Safety

- Work in shade on a cool roof surface.
- Avoid bleach/citrus/petroleum cleaners on soft tops.
- Keep cleaner/protectant off paint and glass as much as possible.

> Warning: "Steer clear of cleaners with bleach, citrus, or petroleum..." https://www.chemicalguys.com/blogs/exterior-how-tos/how-to-clean-and-protect-convertible-tops

## Required Tools

- Soft/medium convertible-top brush
- Microfiber towels
- Low-pressure rinse water
- Soft-top-safe cleaner
- Soft-top waterproofing/protectant

## Required Parts / Fluids

- Convertible top cleaner (material-compatible)
- Fabric/vinyl top protectant per top material

## Model-specific notes (NB1 vs NB2)

- NB1/NB2 may have OEM-style vinyl tops or aftermarket cloth/canvas tops; cleaner/protectant choice must match installed material.
- Always test chemicals on a hidden area first.

> Manual-reference quote: "Welcome to Mazda Miata Factory Service Manuals" https://www.mellens.net/mazda/

> Community quote: "How to deep clean soft top?" https://www.reddit.com/r/Miata/search/?q=NB+soft+top+cleaning&restrict_sr=1

## Step-by-Step Procedure

### 1) Remove loose debris first

Brush/rinse away loose dirt and pollen before scrubbing to reduce fiber abrasion.

### 2) Pre-rinse and apply cleaner

Pre-rinse the top lightly, apply soft-top-safe cleaner evenly, and allow short dwell time per product directions.

> High-risk quote: "Begin by rinsing with water... Use a pH-neutral car shampoo to cleanse" https://stonercarcare.com/blogs/product-tips/clean-convertible-top

### 3) Gentle scrubbing technique

Use a soft/medium brush with controlled pressure. Work in small sections and avoid aggressive scrubbing.

> High-risk quote: "Remove residue using a medium bristled brush ... gentle enough that you will not risk tearing your convertible top." https://stonercarcare.com/blogs/product-tips/clean-convertible-top

### 4) Thorough rinse and complete drying

Rinse away all cleaner residue, then dry with microfiber towels and allow full air dry before applying any waterproofing.

> High-risk quote: "Rinse thoroughly... Soap that remains on the fabric could cause spotting or haziness." https://stonercarcare.com/blogs/product-tips/clean-convertible-top

### 5) Apply waterproofing/protectant in light, even coats

Apply protectant per label instructions. Avoid over-saturation and allow cure time before exposing the top to rain.

> High-risk quote: "Fabric protectants were developed to shield soft convertible tops from liquids, dirt, stains, and UV solar rays." https://stonercarcare.com/blogs/product-tips/clean-convertible-top

### 6) Verify water behavior and inspect seals/drains

After cure, mist-test for water beading/sheeting. If water retention persists in areas, apply a maintenance coat and inspect weather seals/drains.

> Community confirmation quote: "Yesterday I Cleaned and weather proofed the soft top." https://www.reddit.com/r/Miata/search/?q=NB+soft+top+waterproofing&restrict_sr=1

## Verification / Post-service checks

- Top has even color/no cleaner residue.
- Water beads/sheds rather than soaking in quickly.
- No streaking on paint/glass from overspray.
