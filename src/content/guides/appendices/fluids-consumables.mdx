## How to use this appendix

Use this as a **pre-service shopping sheet** for Mazda MX-5 NB (1998-2005). It is designed to eliminate vague "use correct fluid" instructions.

- Buy by **capacity + safety margin** (don’t buy exact-to-the-mL amounts).
- Match fluid **spec first**, then brand.
- If chemistry/spec is unknown in the car, do a full change rather than top-up guesswork.

## NB fluid/spec table (practical workshop baseline)

| System | Spec to target | Typical service amount | What to buy |
|---|---|---:|---:|
| Engine coolant | Ethylene-glycol, aluminum-safe. NB commonly accepts traditional or OAT coolant if not mixed. FL22 only if cap/manual/label requires it. | **~6.0 L total system** | **7 L premix** or **4 L concentrate + 4 L distilled water** |
| Engine oil + filter | API SL/SM+ quality oil. 5W-30 is common NB baseline (adjust by climate/engine wear). | **~3.6 L (3.8 US qt) with filter** | **5 L jug** + filter + crush washer |
| Manual transmission | **GL-4 75W-90** for synchronized NB gearbox (avoid generic GL-5 here unless explicitly synchro-safe by manufacturer data). | **~1.8-2.0 L** | **3 x 1 L / 3 qt** |
| Differential | Hypoid **GL-5 75W-90** (open diff and most stock NB LSD setups). | **~0.6-0.8 L** | **1 x 1 L / 1 qt** |
| Brake system | DOT grade on reservoir cap/manual (DOT 4 commonly used on NB service). | **~0.7-1.0 L full flush** | **1 L sealed bottle** |
| Clutch hydraulic | Same DOT family as brake fluid. | **~0.2-0.3 L clutch-only bleed** | If doing brakes too: one fresh 1 L bottle total |
| Power steering | ATF with **Dexron II/III compatibility** (NB manual-language references: Mazda M-III equivalent). | **~0.7-0.9 L full exchange** | **1 L ATF** |
| Shifter turret (manual cars) | Gear oil for turret service per your transmission setup (commonly GL-4 style MTF in NB owner practice). | **~90-100 mL** | Small bottle / use measured remainder |

## Known-good product examples (non-sponsored)

### Coolant (NA/NB-compatible chemistry choices)
- Comma Super Coldmaster (traditional ethylene-glycol)
- Comma Xstream G30 (OAT long-life)
- Mazda FL22 premix only where explicitly specified on your car

### Manual transmission (GL-4)
- Red Line MT-90 (75W-90 GL-4)
- Other reputable GL-4 75W-90 MTF products that explicitly mention synchronized manual boxes

### Differential (GL-5)
- Red Line 75W90 GL-5
- Motul Gear 300 75W-90 (GL-4/GL-5; acceptable where your diff requires GL-5 behavior)

### Power steering ATF
- Dexron III/Mercon labeled ATF from major brands
- Multi-vehicle ATF explicitly listing Dexron II/III compatibility

## Consumables to keep on the shelf

- Engine sump plug crush washers
- Differential/trans plug washers
- Distilled water (5 L minimum)
- Brake cleaner
- Nitrile gloves
- Spill-free coolant funnel
- Fluid transfer pump for side-fill gearboxes/differentials
- Marking pen + service labels

## Record this every time (high value for diagnostics)

1. Date + odometer
2. Exact product name and viscosity/spec line from bottle
3. Quantity used
4. Any contamination notes (metallic, milky, burnt smell)
5. Next planned interval

Good records make later diagnosis of shift feel, diff whine, cooling behavior, or PS noise dramatically easier.
